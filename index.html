WE ARE

At Pure Perfumes, we are one of the few quality suppliers and packaging masters when it comes to fragrance industry!

We design and develop perfume packaging ideas as well as get the manufacturing done for plastic, metal, glass, wooden and paper packaging components and accessories for prestige and premium brands, for whom quality and innovation come first.

Since our establishment in 2014, we have made a mark as a unique single source supplier of refined quality and innovative packaging for prestige and premium brands. We are differentiated by our end-to-end service – from design – development – manufacturing – quality assurance – packaging – and logistics. We are an ISO 9001 certified company with three development and quality centers, while our Business Development office is in Dubai, UAE. We offer our clients a white glove experience by providing support throughout the package development process. We are more than just a packaging provider. We are a partner you can rely on.

WHY?

PURE PERFUME

We believe that the first impression is the last impression for a new consumer. It is also the lasting impression as the consumer interacts with the product over its lifetime. Hence packaging is instrumental to the success of any product; particularly a luxury perfume product! Packaging creates an impression of the product inside. For these reasons, it is important that, the packaging not only be beautiful but also work flawlessly. Pure Perfumes is dedicated to achieving this perfect balance!
